  • Shelter Business Model

MMS offers several shelter business model options to support your Mexico manufacturing strategy goals. We will be happy to quote whichever model you prefer.

Start-up

Just need help establishing a manufacturing facility in Mexico? For a project fee with cost-plus pricing on actual expenses, our team will provide a complete package of start-up services include: help in establishing your Mexican corporation, accounting setup, HR services, facility lease or purchase negotiation, setup of legal compliance systems, customs support and management of the operation until your team is ready to take over.

Cost-Plus

In this arrangement MMSâ€™ service fee is included in a mutually agreed upon markup on top of actual monthly operating expenses. As part of the quoting process we will provide best estimates of likely operating expenses based on the manning table and operational description you provide.

Fixed Fee

In this arrangement MMS bills a fixed monthly fee to cover services in addition to actual monthly operating expenses. Markup is applied to operating expenses paid by MMS. As part of the quoting process we will provide best estimates of likely operating expenses based on the manning table and operational description you provide.

Shared Shelter

Not quite large enough to want the costs associated with a stand-alone operation? Shared shelter places non-competing companies in a shared building. The cost of common services such as cafeteria and nurse are shared between the companies in the shared shelter. Shared shelters can be quoted cost-plus or fixed fee.

Piece Price Assembly

Need local assembly support for components associated with your operation or simply want to tap the lower cost of Mexican labor without establishing a shelter? MMS can provide assembly services to support your needs on a piece price basis.
