Challenge

Milwaukee Electronics Companies, an electronics manufacturing services provider, was originally subcontracting work to a company in Mexico. In 2002, management decided that it was time to establish its own company in Mexico.

Solution

MMS originally provided a shared shelter solution through a leased facilty. While the management team worked directly for MEC and the company established its own legal presence in Mexico, MMS handles human resources functions, permit and report filing, Mexico accounting, environmental compliance support, employee benefits administration, import/export paperwork, and negotiations with Mexican suppliers. MMS also provides a U.S. warehouse and support personnel for inbound/outbound material storage.

Results

MEC now occupies the entire building it originally shared. Discussions are underway with developers to move to larger building located on a shared campus operated by MMS. MEC is supplying printed circuit board assemblies to another MMS customer.
